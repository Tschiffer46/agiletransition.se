Who We Are

Senior practitioners, not career consultants.

Agile Transition Management AB was founded to bridge the gap between strategy and execution. We are a lean, senior advisory firm based in Skåne, Sweden, that partners with organisations across the Nordics and Europe.

Our team blends deep experience in technology leadership, M&A integration, AI-driven transformation, and executive coaching. We bring people who have sat in your chair — CTOs, transformation leads, and board advisors with decades of hands-on experience.
